# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** October 17, 2025  

CertifyQuiz protects user privacy and processes personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 1. Data we collect
- Name, email, and preferred language  
- Quiz results and earned badges  
- Technical data (IP address, device, browser) collected in aggregated or anonymized form

## 2. Purpose
- Provide access to quizzes and Premium features  
- Improve user experience and personalize content  
- Offer support and service-related communications  

## 3. Data retention
We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

## 4. Your rights
You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by writing to  
**[privacy@certifyquiz.com](mailto:privacy@certifyquiz.com)**.
