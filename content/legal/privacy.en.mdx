# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** October 17, 2025

CertifyQuiz protects user privacy and processes personal data in compliance with GDPR.

## 1. Data we collect
- Name, email, preferred language
- Quiz results and earned badges
- Technical data (IP, device, browser) in aggregated/anonymized form

## 2. Purpose
- Provide access to quizzes and Premium features
- Improve UX and personalize content
- Support and service communications

## 3. Retention
We retain personal data only as long as needed to provide the service, then delete or anonymize it.

## 4. Your rights
You can request access, correction, or deletion at **privacy@certifyquiz.com**.
