# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** October 17, 2025  

CertifyQuiz uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze site traffic, and collect anonymous statistics.

## 1. Types of cookies
- **Technical:** required for the site to function correctly  
- **Analytics:** help us understand usage patterns and improve our services  
- **Third-party:** e.g., Google Analytics, Brevo, LinkedIn Insight

## 2. Managing cookies
You can modify or withdraw your consent at any time via the initial cookie banner or your browser settings.  
Refer to your browser’s “Cookie Management” section for detailed instructions.
